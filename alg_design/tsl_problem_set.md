### Day 2 — Graphs Part 1 (Concept Building)
**Goal:** Solidify BFS vs DFS, get comfortable with core patterns.

| # | Problem | Difficulty | Topic |
|---|---|---|---|
| 13 | [Number of Islands](https://leetcode.com/problems/number-of-islands/submissions/1964632144) | Medium | DFS/BFS |
| 14 | [Clone Graph](https://leetcode.com/problems/clone-graph/submissions/1965264066) | Medium | DFS/BFS |
| 15 | [Course Schedule](https://leetcode.com/problems/course-schedule/submissions/1965381557) | Medium | Cycle Detection |
| 16 | Course Schedule II | Medium | Topological Sort |
| 17 | Pacific Atlantic Water Flow | Medium | BFS/DFS |
| 18 | Word Ladder | Hard | BFS |

**Notes:**
- Do #13 (Number of Islands) **twice** — once with DFS, once with BFS. Best single problem for learning both traversals
- Do #15 and #16 back to back — same problem, small extension
- #18 (Word Ladder) is hard — budget 30–40 min. If you don't solve it, study the BFS shortest-path approach carefully
- End the day: write BFS template, DFS template, and directed graph cycle detection from memory (pseudocode is fine)
